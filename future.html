I want to be an Environmental Scientist




Our Environment


The natural world around us is essential for our survival and well-being. However, Climate change and biodiversity degradation due to pollution are the challenges our environment faces today. WE all have a role to play in protecting the environment. We can create a healthier, more sustainable future for everyone.

Why Environmental Science?


Environmental Science is a field of study that focuses on the preservation of our finite natural resources while introducing environmental technologies that can mitigate the impact on the environment. It helps us to understand different environmental challenges and issues and develop solutions on how to protect Mother Earth.

What does an Environmental Scientist Do?


Environmental scientist has the capacity to provide designs that will be helpful as a planning tool to avoid adverse impact to our environment. Provide solutions and options to avoid the harmful effect of Climate Change, wastes pollution and environmental damages to the community.

Works on improving public health


Environmental scientists work to improve public health by introducing alternative engineering technologies to improve recycling and recovery, proper waste treatment, and disposal.

Provide Environmental Assessments


Environmental Impact Assessments, wherein scientists apply principles from science and engineering to assess any adverse effects of projects, and how it can be addressed through proper design by providing mitigating measures, and treatment, where adverse impacts are expected.

Works on improving the quality of water supply, and on improving the air quality


Provide scientific results through monitoring and addressing the quality of Water Supply through proper treatment, and providing systems to store and maintain water quality. They will also conduct regular monitoring of the concentration of pollutants and the impact on air quality from vehicle emissions, industries, and other companies that emit air and water pollution and generate solid wastes.
